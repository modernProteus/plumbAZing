Promotions & Services Console

A friendly window onto the sheet-CMS. Build a correctly-formatted row, see exactly how it'll look on the site, crop a promo image to spec, and paste the row into the Google Sheet yourself. This page never writes to the sheet, needs no login, and holds no secrets.

Open the Sheet View Live Site Open Render

How this works

  1. The Google Sheet is the source of truth. This console is a helper — it never edits the sheet by itself.
  2. Build a row below, copy it, and paste it into the matching sheet tab.
  3. Click Publish > Publish to site in the sheet's menu. The live site rebuilds in a few minutes.
  4. Fail-safe by design: a bad row or a down endpoint can never blank the live site — it just keeps showing the last good version.
