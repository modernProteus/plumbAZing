Why water pressure matters

Excessive pressure can put unnecessary stress on pipes, faucets, toilets, water heaters, and appliance connections.

A properly functioning PRV can help reduce wear on your plumbing system and support more stable performance throughout the home.

PRV FAQs

A PRV, or pressure reducing valve, helps regulate water pressure entering your home so your plumbing system is not under excessive strain.
High pressure is not always obvious, but repeated leaks, fixture wear, or being told your pressure is elevated are common signs it’s worth checking.
If your pressure is too high, installing or replacing a PRV can help protect your plumbing system and reduce wear over time.
